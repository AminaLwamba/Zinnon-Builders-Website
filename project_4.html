Project Details

Category: Commercial Construction

Date: July 2022 - December 2023

Client: Braun College

Project Name: Braun Knowlegde Hub

Braun Knowledge Hub

The Braun Library Revitalization project is all about blending the rich history of the library with contemporary design and technology. The exterior will be carefully restored to honor its architectural heritage, while integrating energy-efficient materials and lighting that highlight its stately presence. The new facade will be both a nod to the past and a step into the future. Inside, the library will be a marvel of modern design. The interior layout will be reimagined to create open, flexible spaces that encourage collaboration and quiet study alike. Natural light will flood through expansive windows, illuminating comfortable reading nooks and communal work areas. State-of-the-art facilities, including digital media labs and maker spaces, will provide patrons with access to the latest technology and resources.
