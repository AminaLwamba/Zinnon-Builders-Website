Dahlia Bridge

Project Details

Category: Commercial Construction

Date: July 2022 - December 2023

Client: Kenya National Highway Authorities

Project Name: Dahlia Overpass Project

Dahlia Overpass Project

Constructed with sustainable materials, the Dahlia Bridge is not only a marvel of design but also a beacon of environmental consciousness. Solar panels line the upper structure, harnessing the sun's energy to illuminate the bridge at night, casting a soft, enchanting glow over the river. The bridge's support piers are adorned with vertical gardens, which not only add to the visual appeal but also contribute to the local ecosystem by providing habitats for various plant species and pollinators. The Dahlia Bridge is designed with pedestrians and cyclists in mind, offering wide, safe pathways alongside.
